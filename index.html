McDonald's Week Info


McDonalds Week is an annual St. Mark’s event that aims to raise money for Austin Street Shelter. This fundraiser will take place at St. Mark’s from November 15-18. With help from McDonalds and several Preston Royal restaurants, we hope get students involved with this event and ultimately support Austin Street Shelter. Additionally, all throughout this week, we will have events including a karaoke competition, chemistry show, a ping pong tournament. You can also donate by buying raffle tickets, t-shirts, and wristbands during this week. We are honored to partner with the many organizations involved in the event, and we look forward to a successful week!
